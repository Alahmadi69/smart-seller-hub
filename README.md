# Smart Seller Hub

## The Business Problem
Micro-businesses and independent sellers on platforms like Instagram and WhatsApp struggle with managing customer orders manually. Tracking bank transfer receipts, collecting accurate shipping addresses through endless chats, and manually updating inventory leads to human errors, lost sales, and a frustrating customer experience.

## The Proposed Solution
A lightweight, user-friendly order management web application. Sellers can create a simple product link to put in their social media bio. Customers use the link to view products, place orders, enter shipping details, and upload payment receipts. All data is automatically organized in a centralized, easy-to-read dashboard for the seller to process efficiently.
